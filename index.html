How Does ThyraFemme Balance Work?

Understanding how ThyraFemme Balance works requires a brief overview of thyroid physiology. The thyroid produces primarily T4 (thyroxine), an inactive precursor hormone that must be converted to T3 (triiodothyronine) in peripheral tissues to become biologically active. This conversion relies on specific enzymes (deiodinases) that require adequate selenium, zinc, and other cofactors to function. When these cofactors are deficient, even a thyroid producing adequate T4 can result in cellular hypothyroidism because the conversion step fails.
